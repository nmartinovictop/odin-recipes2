Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

The Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwhich is a delicious and easy meal which combines two sweet flavors to make heaven.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread. White bread is traditional, but any will work
  • Peanut Butter (crunchy or creamy)
  • Jelly, any flavor. Jam will also work

Steps

  1. Take one slice of white bread. On one side, cover it with peanut butter
  2. On the other slice of bread, cover one side with jelly
  3. combine the two slices of bread. Have the Peanut Butter meet the Jelly
  4. Enjoy
